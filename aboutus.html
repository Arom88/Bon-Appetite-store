About us

Back in 2008 I was introduced to the unique concept of fruit bouquets. Although it had yet to reach Sudbury, it had proven to be quite popular as gifts and centrepieces in larger cities across Canada. Driven by creativity and motivation, I fell in love with the idea of bringing the same kind of happiness to friends and neighbours right here in Sudbury. With my years of experience in food and service industries I was confident that I would be able to set out and accomplish just that. It all started with my first classic mixed fruit bouquet which has now become the cornerstone of my product line. Since then I have grown our offerings to include chocolate covered strawberry bouquets, themed bouquets for every occasion and have welcomed many opportunities to create something new by request. Weddings, showers, just because, new baby, sympathy, birthdays to name only a few, we have proudly created a fruit bouquet for every occasion.
