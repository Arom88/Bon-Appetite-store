Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we offer delivery to most areas in Calgary. You could call us for more information.

Can I pick up a bouquet?

Yes. Pick up is available at 35 Richard court SW, Calgary. Our bouquets are made to order but we cater to last minute needs and will gladly accommodate.

Can I make changes to a bouquet?

Of course! We always do our best to accommodate to your tastes. Please call to discuss options with someone from our team.

How long do the bouquets last?

Our bouquets are made to order so freshness is always guaranteed. We will always suggest day of use when possible however, if you plan on using it the next day we would suggest leaving it in it’s original packaging and keeping it refrigerated until time of use.Any leftover fruit from bouquets should be removed from skewers and stored in the refrigerator in a sealed container.
